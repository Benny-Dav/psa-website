Color Ratios

A good rule:

70% Navy/Dark Blue
20% White/Light Text
8% Gold
2% Teal/Purple accents

The mistake most people make with logos like this is overusing gold. Gold works best when it's rare. The navy background should dominate, allowing the gold to feel prestigious and intentional.

For a Prophet Selasi Adrah personal ministry website, I'd choose a dark navy + gold luxury theme rather than a bright church-style design. It aligns much better with the logo and creates a stronger premium brand presence.
